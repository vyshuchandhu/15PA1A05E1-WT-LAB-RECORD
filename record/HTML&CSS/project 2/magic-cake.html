Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastest cake has ever known.
Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are all stories.
we also want to stress that whatever happens to you as a result of
consuming Magic Cake is your responsibility.you've been warned,
and you can view our legal page for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs,sugar and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread it to non-stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
